Who We Are

About Hamatoz

A graduation project built to simplify automotive discovery through one unified platform for vehicles and spare parts.

Hamatoz was created to address the fragmentation of the automotive market by bringing buying, selling, exchange, and spare-parts discovery into one modern digital experience.

lightbulb

Why We Built Hamatoz

The automotive market often feels scattered and inefficient. We created Hamatoz to make vehicle and spare parts discovery simpler, more connected, and more trustworthy.

visibility

Our Vision

We aim to build a smarter and more trusted automotive marketplace that makes discovery and communication easier for users.

heart_check

What Drives Us

We believe automotive platforms should be clear, modern, and accessible—with stronger trust, better organization, and a smoother user journey.

The Problem We're Solving

Fragmented Marketplaces

Users often move between different sources to find vehicles, spare parts, and seller information.

Difficult Part Discovery

Finding the right part can be time-consuming without proper categorization and guidance.

Scattered Communication

Buyers and sellers lack one clear, direct, and organized channel for communication.

Limited Trust

Users need more confidence when browsing listings, comparing options, and connecting with sellers.

Our Idea

Hamatoz combines vehicles, spare parts, exchange workflows, and intelligent discovery into one unified web platform. Instead of moving between disconnected sources, users explore a more organized and trusted automotive experience in one place.

Built as a graduation project focused on improving how people explore vehicles and spare parts through one connected digital experience.
